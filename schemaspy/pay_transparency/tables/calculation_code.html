Description

This is a lookup table of all 57 statistics, known as ‘calculations’, that relate to calculating any disparity in gender pay such as the mean hourly pay difference. The webapp has the calculation_code in two places, when calculating the statistics to store in the pay_transparency_calculated_data table, and when adding the statistics in the report. These operations require looking up either the calculation_code_id or the calculation_code from this table.
